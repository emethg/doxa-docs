Doxa maintains a public status page where you can check the real-time operational status of its services and view historical uptime data.

## Access the status page

Visit [status.doxa.com](https://status.doxa.com) to view the current status of Doxa services.

The status page displays the operational status of:

- **Documentation** - Hosted documentation sites
- **Updates** - Documentation update and deployment services
- **Dashboard** - The Doxa dashboard and management interface

## Subscribe to updates

Subscribe to receive notifications when we detect incidents, resolve incidents, and plan scheduled maintenance.

1. Navigate to [status.doxa.com](https://status.doxa.com).
2. Click **Subscribe to updates**.
3. Select your preferred notification method (email, RSS, or RSS via Slack).

## Report issues

If you're experiencing issues that aren't reflected on the status page, contact Doxa support at [support@doxa.com](mailto:support@doxa.com).
